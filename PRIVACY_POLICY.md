# Privacy Policy for Save Up

**Last Updated:** November 16, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Save Up ("Application") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Registration:**
- Email address
- Password (hashed and encrypted)
- Full name (optional)

**Profile Information:**
- Monthly or annual salary amount
- Salary type (monthly/annual)
- Currency preference
- Region/location for currency formatting
- Questionnaire responses (7 questions about spending habits)

**Usage Data:**
- Spending decisions (item names, prices, purchase decisions)
- "Let Me Think" reminders you set
- Decision history and tracking data
- Profile updates and preferences

**Optional Features:**
- Preference to enable/disable buying questionnaire
- Custom waiting period preferences for reminders

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Technical Information:**
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- IP address (through Supabase logs)
- General location (country/region from IP)
- App crash data and error logs

**Usage Analytics:**
- Features you interact with most
- Time spent in the application
- Onboarding completion status

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Services:**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Calculate work hours and investment values
   - Store and display your spending decisions
   - Generate your savings statistics
   - Process "Let Me Think" reminders

2. **Improve the Application:**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Identify and fix bugs
   - Optimize features and performance
   - Understand user preferences

3. **Ensure Security:**
   - Verify your identity
   - Detect fraudulent activities
   - Protect against unauthorized access

4. **Communicate with You:**
   - Send essential notifications (reminders, alerts)
   - Respond to your inquiries
   - Provide customer support

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:
- **Consent**: You explicitly consent by creating an account
- **Contract**: Processing necessary to provide services you requested
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improving security, analytics, and app performance

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where Your Data is Stored
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- **Server Location**: Your data may be stored in different geographic regions depending on Supabase infrastructure
- **Local Storage**: Spending decisions may be cached locally on your device (AsyncStorage)

### 5.2 Security Measures
- All data transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connections
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Database is protected with authentication tokens
- Regular security audits (as provided by Supabase)
- Data is never shared with third parties

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as long as needed to provide services to you. 

**Specific Retention Periods:**
- Account information: Until account deletion
- Spending decisions: Until manually deleted or account deleted
- Local cache data: Until app is uninstalled
- Error logs: 30 days

## 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 7.1 Access and Portability
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Receive data in a portable format

### 7.2 Correction
- Update or correct inaccurate information
- Done through the app's Profile settings

### 7.3 Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
- Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
- Contact: [support email] with subject "Account Deletion Request"
- We will delete all your data within 30 days

### 7.4 Opt-Out
- Disable optional features (buying questionnaire, analytics)
- Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing

## 8. Children's Privacy

Save Up is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information and terminate the child's account.

By using Save Up, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside of your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.
